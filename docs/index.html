Workflow

How Echo works.

From your voice to Claude-ready text in four simple steps, one keypress.

  1. 1

    Record

    Press ⌘⇧V or hold fn and start talking, in any app.

  2. 2

    Transcribe

    Local Whisper converts speech to text with high accuracy.

  3. 3

    Refine

    An LLM cleans it up with context from your apps and vocabulary.

  4. 4

    Insert

    Clean, structured text lands at your cursor, Claude-ready.
